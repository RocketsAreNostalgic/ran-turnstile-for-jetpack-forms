# RAN Turnstile artwork studies — 19 July 2026

These are original RocketAreNostalgic visual studies for **RAN Turnstile for
Jetpack Forms**. They are drafts only and must not replace the final
WordPress.org artwork until approved.

- `form-signal-observatory/` — alternative banner concept retained as a draft.
- `human-signal-checkpoint/` — alternative banner concept retained as a draft.
